# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **end-to-end Business Intelligence solution** developed using **Power BI** to analyze the Global Superstore dataset. The objective is to transform raw business data into interactive dashboards that enable business stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed data-driven decisions.

The solution includes **five interactive dashboards** covering Executive Performance, Sales Analysis, Customer Insights, Product Performance, and Geographic Analysis.

---

# 💼 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance through KPIs.
- Analyze monthly sales trends.
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Analyze regional sales and profitability.
- Track business growth using interactive dashboards.
- Support strategic decision-making through business insights.
